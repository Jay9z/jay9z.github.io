Flip Cards(XMAS Version)

#Play Rules

  1. Turn over any two cards.
  2. If the two cards match, they will be hided.
  3. If they don’t match, them will be turned back over automatically.
  4. Remember what was on each card and where it was.
  5. The game is over when all the cards have been matched.
  6. Do you best to remember every card and minimize your flip times
